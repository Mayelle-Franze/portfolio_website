Bitu - Responsive Web App

Designing a bike-trip App

UI / UX Design Process

Bitu is a responsive app from scratch for intuitive planning for your next cycling trip. The process started with UX research, where the concept was created, the user-defined,and the goal formulated. Subsequently, user personas were created and wireframes were sketched based on their needs and wants. Afterward, testing eliminated design errors and generated a user-friendly and intuitive design. Once the style guide was set the final design was created.
